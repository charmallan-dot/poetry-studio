Poetry Styles

Master every form with our AI assistance

"cherry blossoms fall
dancing on the spring breeze now
pink snow covers grass"

Haiku

Traditional 5-7-5 syllable structure. Capture moments in nature.

"Shall I compare thee to a summer's day?
Thou art more lovely and more temperate..."

Sonnet

Shakespearean 14-line form. Perfect for love and deep emotions.

"the city never sleeps, they said
but i found silence at 4am
on the rooftop watching streetlights..."

Free Verse

Contemporary, unrhymed poetry. Express yourself freely.

"From the bottom we rise / Through the struggle, eyes on the prize
Every verse a testimony, every line a sacrifice..."

Rap

Authentic hip-hop verses with flow, wordplay, and hooks.
